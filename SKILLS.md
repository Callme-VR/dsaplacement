## 🧠 Algorithmic Patterns

### 1. Hashing & Frequency Counting
- **Concepts**: $O(1)$ average time complexity for lookups and insertions.
- **Applied in**: `Two Sum`, `Valid Anagram`, `Group Anagrams`.

### 2. Two Pointers
- **Concepts**: Reducing $O(n^2)$ search space to $O(n)$ by leveraging sorted properties or meeting-in-the-middle.
- **Applied in**: `3Sum`, `4Sum`, `Valid Palindrome`.

### 3. Sliding Window
- **Concepts**: Dynamic and fixed-size windows to process contiguous subarrays or substrings efficiently.
- **Applied in**: `Sliding Window Maximum`, `Longest Substring Without Repeating Characters`.

### 4. Bit Manipulation
- **Concepts**: Using bitwise operators (`&`, `|`, `^`, `<<`, `>>`) for high-performance, low-level computations.
- **Applied in**: `Number of 1 Bits`, `Count Bits`.
